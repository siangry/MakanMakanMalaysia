Restaurant Guide

Guidance for the rich flavors and diverse culinary traditions of Malaysia

Nur Nasuha Tomyam Seafood

KAMPAR, PERAK

Rating: ★★★★☆

Review: Nur Nasuha Tomyam Seafood offers delicious affordable fried rice, making it a top choice for those seeking flavorful dishes at unbeatable prices.

Yew Kei Seafood Restaurant

TUALANG, PERAK

Rating: ★★★★☆

Review: Yew Kei Seafood Restaurant is renowned for its incredibly fresh seafood, offering a delightful dining experience with dishes that capture the true essence of the ocean. Perfect for seafood lovers seeking quality and flavor in every bite

Kedai Kopi Lung Ching

SIBU, SARAWAK

Rating: ★★★★☆

Review: Kedai Kopi Lung Ching is beloved for its local cuisine, especially the unique Sarawak dishes like kampua and golo mee. A standout is the rice noodle with pickled vegetables(Zāo cài fěn gàn), drawing many visitors with its authentic flavors and affordable prices.

Gem Restaurant Damansara

PETALING JAYA, SELANGOR

Rating: ★★★★★

Review: Gem Restaurant Damansara delivers a fantastic dining experience with superb Indian cuisine, attentive service, and great value. The authentic flavors and reasonable prices, make it a must-visit for Indian food lovers.

Ho Kow Hainam Kopitiam

KUALA LUMPUR

Rating: ★★★★★

Review: Enjoyed a delicious breakfast with friends at this popular spot. Great oriental decor, generous portions, and light, flavorful bread. 5 people spend around RM65 only - Good Value!

Lepau Restaurant

KUCHING, SARAWAK

Rating: ★★★★★

Review: Lepau Restaurant offers an authentic aboriginal dining experience with a wide variety of unique dishes. While the flavors may be challenging for some tourists, it's a must-visit for adventurous eaters, especially for those curious to try their signature dish—sago worm!

De.Wan 1958 by Chef Wan

KUALA LUMPUR

Rating: ★★★★★

Review: This is a Famous Malay Restaurant, you can had a memorable company lunch here, enjoying fresh and generously sized seafood along with tender chicken satay. The restaurant's beautiful ambiance is perfect for photos, though some dishes can be quite spicy. Overall, it's an excellent spot for group dining

Nur Nasuha Tomyam Seafood - Suggested Menu

Fried Rice: A delicious, flavorful fried rice dish that's a favorite among locals and visitors alike.

Tomyam Soup: A spicy and sour soup with fresh seafood, perfect for warming up your palate.

Omelette with Stir-fried Chicken: A hearty dish combining a fluffy omelette with savory stir-fried chicken, topped with a rich sauce.

Yew Kei Seafood Restaurant - Suggested Menu

Steamed Prawns with Garlic and Ginger: These fresh prawns are steamed to perfection, preserving their natural sweetness. Enhanced with aromatic garlic and ginger, the dish is light yet bursting with flavor, making it a must-try for seafood enthusiasts.

Salted Egg Crab: Experience the rich and indulgent taste of crab coated in a creamy salted egg yolk sauce. The crabs are perfectly cooked, with a golden crust that adds a delightful crunch to each bite.

Kam Heong Lala (Clams): A popular Malaysian dish, these clams are stir-fried with a fragrant blend of curry leaves, dried shrimp, and spices. The result is a savory, slightly spicy, and incredibly flavorful dish that seafood lovers will adore.

Kedai Kopi Lung Ching - Suggested Menu

Kampua: A traditional Foochow noodle dish served either dry (white) or with soy sauce (black). The noodles are topped with slices of char siu, spring onions, and fried shallots for a savory and satisfying meal.

Zao Cai Feng Gan: A unique and flavorful soup made with preserved mustard greens, vermicelli noodles, and minced meat. This dish offers a tangy and comforting taste that is perfect for any time of the day.

Kompia: A traditional Foochow bagel-like bread, usually stuffed with savory minced pork. It's crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, making it a popular snack or breakfast item in Sarawak.

Gem Restaurant Damansara - Suggested Menu

Biryani: A flavorful and aromatic rice dish cooked with a blend of spices, tender meat, and garnished with fresh herbs. Served with raita and curry, this biryani is a hearty and satisfying meal.

Paneer Butter Masala: A rich and creamy curry made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a tomato-based sauce with butter and a blend of aromatic spices. Perfect with naan or rice.

Sizzling Brownie with Ice Cream: A decadent dessert featuring a warm brownie served on a sizzling plate, topped with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and drizzled with chocolate sauce. A delightful way to end your meal.

Ho Kow Hainam Kopitiam - Suggested Menu

Roti Bakar with Kaya: A classic Malaysian breakfast dish featuring toasted bread served with a generous spread of kaya (coconut jam) and butter. Perfectly crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, this dish is a must-try for anyone visiting a traditional kopitiam.

Nasi Lemak with Fried Chicken: Malaysia's national dish served with fragrant coconut rice, crispy fried chicken, boiled egg, peanuts, anchovies, cucumber, and spicy sambal. A hearty and flavorful meal that's sure to satisfy.

Lepau Restaurant - Suggested Menu

Umai: A traditional Melanau dish made from raw fish marinated in lime juice, chilies, and onions. This refreshing dish is often served as an appetizer and offers a tangy and spicy burst of flavors.

Sago Worm: A unique delicacy from Sarawak, sago worms are typically grilled or fried and served with a spicy dipping sauce. This adventurous dish is a must-try for those looking to experience something truly different.

De.Wan 1958 by Chef Wan - Suggested Menu

Grilled Lobster: A luxurious dish featuring perfectly grilled lobster, served with a tangy dipping sauce. The lobster is tender and succulent, with a smoky flavor that pairs beautifully with the fresh lime and chili garnish.

Grilled Fish: A traditional Malay dish, this grilled fish is marinated in a blend of spices and herbs, then cooked to perfection over an open flame. Served with slices of lime and onions, it's a flavorful and satisfying meal.

Chicken Satay: Skewers of marinated chicken grilled to perfection and served with a rich peanut sauce. This dish is a favorite among locals and visitors alike, offering a delightful combination of sweet, savory, and spicy flavors.
